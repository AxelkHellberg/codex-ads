# Ads Audit

Use for multi-platform health checks. Require at least one platform payload, screenshot set, or pasted metric block.

Preferred local command:

```bash
python3 scripts/ads.py audit --input <portfolio.json> --output-dir .
```

Keep the write-up ordered as: score, critical blockers, quick wins, then platform notes.
